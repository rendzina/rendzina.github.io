Isle of Wight Tourist Attractions

1. Osborne House

The former summer residence of Queen Victoria and Prince Albert.

2. The Needles

A famous coastal landmark featuring three chalk stacks.

3. Carisbrooke Castle

A historic castle with a medieval keep, associated with Charles I.

4. Shanklin Chine

A picturesque gorge with a waterfall, offering a scenic walk.

5. Blackgang Chine

One of the UK's oldest amusement parks with themed areas.

6. Ryde Pier

The oldest pier in the country, offering stunning coastal views.

7. Ventnor Botanic Garden

A botanical garden showcasing a variety of plants from around the world.

8. Isle of Wight Steam Railway

A vintage steam train journey through the picturesque countryside.

9. Brading Roman Villa

An archaeological site preserving the remains of a Roman villa.

10. Dinosaur Isle

A museum dedicated to the island's rich fossil heritage.
